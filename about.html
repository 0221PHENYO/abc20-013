About Antalya City

History of Antalya

Antalya was founded by Attalus the II(second) King Pergamum in 159 BC. He
ordered his man to find an earthly paradise and the city was named after him
"Antalia". Antalya is been the center of attraction to different civilizations
From ancient past. It has been famed as the "Heaven on earth" from as long back
as 1st Century BC after Pergamum King Attalus discoverd it. This natural elegant
place was only bettered by consecutive arrivals of different races. For instance,
The Romans who lent their own touch to the city followed him.The Byzantines,
Seljuk Turks and Ottomans all flourished in this ancient city and enriched it.


Is the center culture, Art, Architecture, Mythlogy throught its history. With
its nature made out dark blue seas, Spectacular Tourism,Taurus Moutains, Fervent
Waterfall, colourfull trees, Award-winning International marina, World known
holidays, Village and nature surrounded by the smell of citrus trees is what
makes Antalya the capital of Tourism.
